ยง412:2-603 Disclosures of records of Hawaii financial institutions. Any institution-affiliated party who, without authorization, knowingly discloses, except in the regular course of business, any information derived from a Hawaii financial institution’s records shall be guilty of a misdemeanor punishable pursuant to sections 706-663 and 706-640.
