201-8

Recommendations to agencies

§201-8 Recommendations to agencies. On the basis of submitted reports and such other information as the department of business, economic development, and tourism may obtain, it may, as it deems it desirable for the best coordination and effectiveness of economic development activities in the State, make recommendations in writing to any agency of the state government. [L 1955, c 264, pt of §1; RL 1955, §28A-8; am L 1957, c 23, §7; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §§10, 25; am L 1963, c 2, §3; HRS §201-8; am L 1987, c 336, §7; am L 1990, c 293, §8]
