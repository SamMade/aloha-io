§205-15 Conflict. Except as specifically provided by this chapter and the rules adopted thereto, neither the authority for the administration of chapter 183C nor the authority vested in the counties under section 46-4 shall be affected. [L 1963, c 205, pt of §2; Supp, §98H-15; HRS §205-15; am L 1995, c 11, §11 and c 69, §11]
