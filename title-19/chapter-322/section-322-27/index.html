322-27

Plans, etc.

§322-27 Plans, etc. The comptroller shall transmit to the board with each appeal the appropriate map or plan (or a copy thereof) of the lands to be improved prepared in accordance with the requirements of section 322-22, and shall also transmit to the board with each appeal a statement of the matters and things required by section 322-22 to be noted upon or to accompany the map.
