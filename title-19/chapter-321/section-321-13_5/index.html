321-13.5

Certified nurse aides; investigations of abuse and neglect

[ยง321-13.5 Certified nurse aides; investigations of abuse and neglect.] The department shall adopt rules pursuant to chapter 91 relating to:

(1) The investigation of:

(A) Abuse or neglect by a certified nurse aide working in a health care setting licensed or certified by the department; and

(B) Misappropriation of an individual’s property by a certified nurse aide working in a health care setting licensed or certified by the department; and

(2) Action taken against a certified nurse aide as a result of an investigation pursuant to paragraph (1). [L 2007, c 226, ยง2]
