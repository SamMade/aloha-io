342L-32.5

Delivery, deposit, and acceptance prohibition

[ยง342L-32.5] Delivery, deposit, and acceptance prohibition. No person shall deliver to, deposit into, or accept a regulated substance into an underground storage tank regulated under this chapter at a facility that has been identified by the department to be ineligible for such delivery, deposit, or acceptance. The delivery prohibition may go into effect at a facility before a hearing is conducted by the director. [L 2009, c 7, ยง2]
