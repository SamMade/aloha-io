Reimbursement for inspection

[ยง159-32] Reimbursement for inspection. The department may perform inspection of meat and meat products derived from exotic animals when requested by persons desiring such inspection. Persons requesting and receiving inspection service under this part shall reimburse the State for all costs incurred by the State in the provision of the service.
