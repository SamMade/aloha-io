142-95

Rabbits, Belgian hares, to be kept off ground; penalty

§142-95 Rabbits, Belgian hares, to be kept off ground; penalty. Any person who breeds, raises or keeps rabbits or Belgian hares shall keep them off the ground.

Any person who violates this section shall be fined not more than $100 or imprisoned not more than six months, or both. [L 1957, c 298, §§1, 2; Supp, §20-94; HRS §142-95]
