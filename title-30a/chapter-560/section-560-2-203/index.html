560:2-203

Composition of the augmented estate

ยง560:2-203 Composition of the augmented estate. Subject to section 560:2-208, the value of the augmented estate, to the extent provided in sections 560:2-204, 560:2-205, 560:2-206, and 560:2-207, consists of the sum of the values of all property, whether real or personal; movable or immovable, tangible or intangible, wherever situated, that constitute the decedent’s net probate estate, the decedent’s nonprobate transfers to others, the decedent’s nonprobate transfers to the surviving spouse or reciprocal beneficiary, and the surviving spouse’s or reciprocal beneficiary’s property and nonprobate transfers to others.
