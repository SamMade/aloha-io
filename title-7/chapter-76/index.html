Note

This chapter was extensively revised by L 2000, c 253.

Prior history.

This chapter is based upon L 1955, c 274, ยง1, which completely reenacted RL 1945, chapter 2. Chapter 2 was based upon L 1939, c 187, and was amended by L 1947, cc 110, 119; L Sp 1949, cc 4, 45; L 1951, cc 155, 262, 319; L 1953, c 212. Source notes following each section commence as of L 1955, c 274.

This chapter is revised to conform to L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, ยง11, which changed the state civil service administration by vesting in the state director of personnel services the administration of the state department of personnel services, including the authority to prescribe rules and regulations, and established the state civil service commission as an appellate body.

The law is further revised as superseded by the provisions of the county charters.
