88-111

Custodian of the funds

§88-111 Custodian of the funds. The State director of finance shall be the custodian of the several funds. All payment from the funds shall be made by the director of finance only upon vouchers signed by the chairperson and countersigned by such other person as may be designated by the board of trustees.
