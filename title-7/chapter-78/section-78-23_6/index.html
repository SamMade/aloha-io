Leave of absence to serve as bone marrow donor or organ donor

[ยง78-23.6] Leave of absence to serve as bone marrow donor or organ donor. Any officer or employee in the service of the State or any of the several counties shall be entitled to:

(1) Seven days of paid leave each calendar year to serve as a bone marrow donor; and

(2) Thirty days of paid leave each calendar year to serve as an organ donor. [L 2005, c 176, ยง1]
