Consideration for issuance of stocks and bonds

ยง53-28 Consideration for issuance of stocks and bonds. No redevelopment corporation shall issue stock, bonds, or income debenture certificates except for money or property actually received for the use and lawful purposes of the redevelopment corporation. No stock, bonds, or income debenture certificates shall be issued for property except upon a valuation approved by the redevelopment agency and the valuation shall be used in computing actual or estimated cost.
