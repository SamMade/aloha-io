47-21

Certificate furnished garnishee

ยง47-21 Certificate furnished garnishee. After final judgment or determination of the action, the party prevailing shall obtain from the court by which the final judgment or determination was made, a certificate, which shall sufficiently describe the action to apprise the garnishee of its identity, and shall state the nature and amount of the final judgment or determination made therein, and the certificate shall be immediately furnished to the garnishee.
