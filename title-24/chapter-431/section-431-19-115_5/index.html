431:19-115.5

Applicability of other laws to captive insurance

§431:19-115.5 Applicability of other laws to captive insurance companies writing motor vehicle insurance policies in this State. Captive insurance companies writing motor vehicle insurance policies in this State shall be subject to sections 431:10C-102, 431:10C-103, 431:10C-107, 431:10C-108, 431:10C-109, 431:10C-112, 431:10C-115, 431:10C-119, 431:10C-120, 431:10C-207, 431:10C-211, 431:10C-212, 431:10C-213, 431:10C-215, 431:10C-301, and 431:10C-303 through 431:10C-315. Captive insurance companies shall also be subject to the rules adopted by the commissioner to implement these sections. [L 1993, c 205, pt of §1; am L 1997, c 251, §56; am L 1999, c 163, §14]
