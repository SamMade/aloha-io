431:20-103

General insurance law applicable

§431:20-103 General insurance law applicable. The following provisions shall apply to title insurance and to title insurers:

(1) Sections 431:1-103 and 431:1-105;

(2) Sections 431:1-212, 431:1-213, and 431:1-214;

(3) Sections 431:2-101 to 431:2-106, and sections 431:2-108 to 431:2-110;

(4) Sections 431:2-201 to 431:2-204, sections 431:2-207 to 431:2-212, and section 431:2-215;

(5) Sections 431:2-302, 431:2-303, 431:2-305, and 431:2-306;

(6) Sections 431:3-101 to 431:3-105;

(7) Sections 431:3-201 to 431:3-203, 431:3-205, and 431:3-206, and sections 431:3-209 to 431:3-220;

(8) Sections 431:3-301, 431:3-305, 431:3-307, and 431:3-308;

(9) Sections 431:4-102 to 431:4-127;

(10) Sections 431:4-202 to 431:4-207;

(11) Section 431:5-101;

(12) Sections 431:5-201 to 431:5-203;

(13) Sections 431:5-305 and 431:5-306, and sections 431:5-308 to 431:5-311;

(14) Article 6;

(15) Article 7;

(16) Article 9A;

(17) Sections 431:10-211, 431:10-216 to 431:10-218, and 431:10-220, 431:10-221, and 431:10-224, 431:10-225, and sections 431:10-235 to 431:10-238;

(18) Article 13; and

(19) Article 15. [L 1987, c 347, pt of §2; am L 1988, c 363, §2(4); am L 2002, c 155, §96; am L 2003, c 212, §117; am L 2006, c 154, §41]
