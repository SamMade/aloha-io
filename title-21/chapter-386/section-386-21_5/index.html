386-21.5

Publication of fees by prepaid health care plan contractors

[ยง386-21.5] Publication of fees by prepaid health care plan contractors. (a) A prepaid health care plan contractor as defined in section 393-3 shall provide the director upon request with a schedule of all the maximum allowable medical fees.

(b) Pursuant to section 386-21©, the director shall review and, to the extent possible, shall use the fee obtained under subsection (a) as the primary guideline in establishing prevalent charges for medical care, services, and supplies in adopting the fee schedule for workers’ compensation claims.
