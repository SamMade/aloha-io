372-6

Related instruction

§372-6 Related instruction. Related instruction for apprentices and training of teachers and coordinators for the instruction shall be the responsibility of the community colleges of the University of Hawaii system. [L Sp 1941, c 23, §2; RL 1945, §4143; am L 1955, c 31, §1(a); RL 1955, §89-6; am L 1965, c 138, §1; am L 1967, c 20, §1(e); HRS §372-6; am L 1990, c 101, §1; am L 2012, c 14, §7]
