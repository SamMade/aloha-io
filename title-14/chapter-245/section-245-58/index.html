245-58

Illegal sale of export or foreign cigarettes; revocation or suspension of license; civil penalties

[ยง245-58] Illegal sale of export or foreign cigarettes; revocation or suspension of license; civil penalties. If any licensee violates this part or any rule adopted pursuant to this part, the director may:

(1) Revoke or suspend the licensee’s license pursuant to procedures complying with chapter 91; and

(2) Impose a civil penalty in an amount not to exceed the greater of five times the retail value of the cigarettes involved, or $5,000. [L 2000, c 201, pt of ยง1]
