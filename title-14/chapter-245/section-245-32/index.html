245-32

Tax refund or credit for cigarettes and tobacco products shipped for sale or use outside the State

[ยง245-32] Tax refund or credit for cigarettes and tobacco products shipped for sale or use outside the State. (a) The department shall adopt rules to provide a tobacco tax refund or credit to a licensee who has paid a tobacco tax on the distribution of cigarettes or tobacco products that are shipped to a point outside the State for subsequent sale or use outside the State.

(b) This part shall not apply to cigarettes or tobacco products that are distributed in this State to consumers and that are subsequently taken outside the State. [L 2000, c 249, pt of ยง1]
