674-4

Tenure and compensation of members

ยง674-4 Tenure and compensation of members. The term of office of each member of the panel shall be until December 31, 1999. Any member appointed to fill a vacancy shall be appointed by the governor for the remainder of the term. A vacancy in the panel shall not affect its powers.

Each member of the panel shall be compensated at the rate of $100 per day for each day’s actual attendance to the member’s duties; provided that the compensation shall not exceed a maximum of $10,000 per year. The members of the panel shall be paid their necessary traveling and subsistence expenses incurred in the discharge of their duties. Expenses incurred under this section shall be paid by the department of commerce and consumer affairs. [L 1991, c 323, pt of ยง1; am L 1993, c 351, ยง2; am L 1997, c 382, ยง4]
