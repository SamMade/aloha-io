Certificate of boundaries, fee

§664-3 Certificate of boundaries, fee. The certificates of each commissioner of boundaries shall be made on stamped paper, furnished by the department of land and natural resources, and each commissioner of boundaries shall collect and account to the department for the benefit of the public treasury, $1 for each stamped certificate issued by the commissioner. [L 1894-5, c 14, §9; RL 1925, §554; RL 1935, §3662; RL 1945, §10203; RL 1955, §234-3; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §21; am L 1961, c 132, §1; HRS §664-3; gen ch 1985]
