658H-11

International commercial mediation

[ยง658H-11] International commercial mediation. (a) Except as provided in subsections (b) and ©, if a mediation is an international commercial mediation, the mediation is governed by the model law.

(b) Unless the mediation parties agree in accordance with section 658H-3© that all or part of an international commercial mediation is not privileged, sections 658H-4, 658H-5, and 658H-6 and any applicable definitions in section 658H-2 also apply to the mediation and nothing in article 10 of the model law derogates from sections 658H-4, 658H-5, and 658H-6.
