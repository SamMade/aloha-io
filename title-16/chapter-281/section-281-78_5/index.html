281-78.5

Practices to promote excessive consumption of liquor; prohibited

[ยง281-78.5] Practices to promote excessive consumption of liquor; prohibited. (a) No person licensed to sell liquor for consumption on the premises shall engage in practices which promote excessive consumption of liquor.

(b) The liquor commission shall adopt rules pursuant to chapter 91 to prohibit specific liquor promotion practices which promote excessive consumption of liquor.
