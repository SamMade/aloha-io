461-3

Records

§461-3 Records. The executive secretary, subject to the direction of the board, shall make and keep all records and record books required to be kept by the board and shall furnish the department of health with copies of those records as it requires. [L 1949, c 175, pt of §1; RL 1955, §71-3; am L 1957, c 152, §1; am L Sp 1959 2d, c 1, §§14, 15, 19; am L 1961, c 184, §15; am L 1963, c 114, §§1, 3; HRS §461-3; am L 1983, c 23, §1; gen ch 1985; am L 1986, c 143, §5; am L 1992, c 202, §136]
