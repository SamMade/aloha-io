302A-427

Powers of department

[ยง302A-427] Powers of department. No license shall be issued under sections 302A-424 to 302A-428 until the department has approved the method and content of the advertising, the standards and the methods of instruction, and the equipment provided. The department may consult with trade or vocational experts as to the equipment provided and the standards and methods of instruction offered. The department may adopt reasonable rules relating to the enforcement of sections 302A-424 to 302A-428. [L 1996, c 89, pt of ยง2]
