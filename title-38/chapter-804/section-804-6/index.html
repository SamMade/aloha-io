804-6

Bail bond after conviction

§804-6 Bail bond after conviction. Unless otherwise ordered by the court the bail bond given by any defendant prior to the defendant’s conviction, shall, in cases where bail after conviction is permitted either absolutely or by order of the court, be continued as the bail of the defendant after conviction, and until the final determination of any subsequent proceedings in the cause. [L 1892, c 32, §4; RL 1925, §3981; RL 1935, §5435; RL 1945, §10736; RL 1955, §256-6; HRS §709-6; ren L 1972, c 9, pt of §1; gen ch 1985]

Rules of Court

Bail; bond, see HRPP rule 46.

Case Notes

Condition of bond; notice to principals. 19 H. 5 (1908).
