846-5

Reporting of dispositions

ยง846-5 Reporting of dispositions. It shall be the responsibility of every criminal justice agency in this State to report to the data center the disposition of cases which enter their area in the administration of criminal justice to insure that all systems maintained in this State shall contain complete and accurate criminal history record information. All dispositions shall be reported as promptly as feasible but not later than ninety days after the happening of an event which constitutes a disposition. [L 1979, c 129, pt of ยง2]
