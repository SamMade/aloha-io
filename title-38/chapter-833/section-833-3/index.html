833-3

Order of return to demanding court

ยง833-3 Order of return to demanding court. The prosecuting attorney shall appear at the hearing and report to the district judge the results of the prosecuting attorney’s investigation. If the district judge finds that the affiant is a designated agent of the demanding court, judge, or magistrate and that the person whose removal is sought was released from custody by the demanding court, judge, or magistrate, and that the person has violated the terms or conditions of the person’s release, the district judge may issue an order authorizing the return of the person to the custody of the demanding court, judge or magistrate forthwith.
