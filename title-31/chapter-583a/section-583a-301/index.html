583A-301

Uniform Child-Custody Jurisdiction and Enforcement Act

PART III. ENFORCEMENT

[ยง583A-301] Definitions. In this part:

“Petitioner” means a person who seeks enforcement of an order for return of a child under the Hague Convention on the Civil Aspects of International Child Abduction, or enforcement of a child-custody determination.

“Respondent” means a person against whom a proceeding has been commenced for enforcement of an order for return of a child under the Hague Convention on the Civil Aspects of International Child Abduction, or enforcement of a child-custody determination. [L 2002, c 124, pt of ยง2]
