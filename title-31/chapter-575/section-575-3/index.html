Complaint

§575-3 Complaint. Proceedings under this chapter may be instituted upon complaint made under oath or affirmation by the spouse, child, or children, or either of them, by the child support enforcement agency, or by any other person or persons, or organization, against any person guilty of either of the above named offenses. [L 1913, c 83, §2; RL 1925, §3016; RL 1935, §4502; RL 1945, §12253; RL 1955, §328-3; HRS §575-3; am L 1984, c 250, pt of §1; am L 1986, c 332, §14]
